Acoustics Simulation Techniques

Existing Software

Room acoustics simulation is not a new topic of research. In 1968, Krokstad, Strøm and Sørsdal published their paper entitled "Calculating acoustical room response by use of a ray tracing technique", presenting the first use of computerized ray tracing to find the impulse response of a 3D model [@krokstad1968].
